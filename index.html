📲 Install Amul Calc

Install this app on your device for the best experience — works offline too!

📱 iPhone / iPad (Safari)
  1. Tap the Share button (box with arrow up) in Safari
  2. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  3. Tap Add — done!
🤖 Android (Chrome)
  1. Tap the ⋮ menu in Chrome
  2. Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
  3. Tap Install — done!
